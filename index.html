Blended ROAS (hero, all sources)

Total CM revenue from every channel divided by total Meta ad spend. This is the true business return — it includes organic, Google, email, and all other sources. Matches what ClickMagick shows at the top of its dashboard.

Meta Ads Manager (steel blue)

Purchases and revenue reported by Meta using a 7-day click + 1-day view window. Meta attributes conversions that happened days after the ad interaction. Tends to over-attribute. Compare with CM Meta-only for a clearer picture.

Google Ads (sea green)

ClickMagick last-click data for Google-sourced conversions. Google Ads API integration is coming soon — when live, it will show spend and impression data alongside CM conversion data for direct Google ROAS comparison.

Timezone note

All dates use the Meta ad account timezone shown in the header. Today and Yesterday are calculated in that timezone to match Meta Ads Manager exactly. Data updates hourly — last update shown in the header.
