Meta Ads Manager (steel blue)

Purchases and revenue from Meta using a 7-day click + 1-day view attribution window. Meta attributes a conversion if the customer clicked or viewed an ad in that window — even if they later converted via email or organic. Tends to over-attribute revenue to Meta.

ClickMagick — last-click (magenta)

ClickMagick records the last paid click before each purchase across all channels. More conservative than Meta. CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) = spend divided by purchases. Together Meta and CM give upper and lower bounds on true attribution.

Blended ROAS (channel table)

All CM revenue across every source divided by Meta ad spend. Includes organic, Google, email and other channels alongside Meta — not purely a Meta attribution figure. This matches what ClickMagick shows at dashboard level.

Timezone note

All dates use the Meta ad account timezone shown in the header pill. Today and Yesterday are calculated in that timezone to match Meta Ads Manager exactly. Today shows partial data — it updates hourly. Last update time is shown in the header.
